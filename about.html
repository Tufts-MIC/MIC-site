About the Tufts Machine Intelligence Community

We aim to form a cummunity at Tufts to foster interest and involvement in deep learning and artificial intelligence.

Tufts offeres several courses on aspects of broader machine learning, but currently no focussed progression or concentration on modern deep learning.

We will have weekly meetings to work on projects and workshops to learn more. A big part of our goal involves the other Machine Intelligence Communities at MIT, BU and Harvard.
